A distance metric $d(\cdot,\cdot)$ should satisfy the metric properties:
- non negativity: $d(A,B)≥0$
- Identity: $d(A,B)=0 \iff A=B$
- Symmetry: $d(A,B)=d(B,A)$
- Triangle inequality: $d(A,C)≤d(A,B)+d(B,C)$
