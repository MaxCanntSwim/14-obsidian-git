[[Miscellaneous R7L]]
* G = (V, E)
	* V is the set of vertices(nodes)
	* E is the set of edges
	* Example (undirected):
		* G1 = ({s, a, b, t}, {{s, t}, {s, b}, {a, b}, {b, t}})

	* Example (directed):
		* G2 = ({s, a, b, t}, {(s, t),(s, b),(a, b),(b, t)})
![[Screenshot 2023-01-08 at 15.04.01.jpg]]
* a Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG)
	* no loops
